ATHENAEUM INTERACTIVE

AthenaInt or Athenaeum Interactive is a HTML-based Academic Data and Reference Bank having MySQL as its server database. It is created to help SHS students of ACLC College Tacloban to obtain different learning materials for free, is accessible anytime and anywhere!
